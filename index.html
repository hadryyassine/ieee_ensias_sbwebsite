About us

Who're we

IEEE is the largest professional and technological non profit association globally, with more than 419.000 members. Branching out into several branches from over 160 countries , Our student branch in ENSIAS is the most active one in Morocco .

IEEE ENSIAS Student Branch aims to promote scientific knowledge in the field of engineering. Via workshops, conferences and competitions organized by our club during the university year, we work on preparing our members and our loyal admirators to the global working market.
